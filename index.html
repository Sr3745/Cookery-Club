Unleash Your Creativity!

The Cookery Club invites all food enthusiasts to showcase their creativity through words! Bring out your innovative ideas and pen down unique recipes that inspire taste and imagination.

Competition Rules

  • Originality: Recipes must be original and created during the competition.
  • Time Limit: You will have 30 minutes to complete your recipe.
  • Recipe Format: Include a title, list of ingredients, and clear, step-by-step instructions.
  • Theme: The recipe must match the theme announced at the start of the contest.
  • Judging: Entries will be judged on creativity, clarity, feasibility, and presentation.
  • Pass Collection: Collect your class relieving pass from Convenor Greeshma M. Nair, Department of Commerce, the day before the program.
